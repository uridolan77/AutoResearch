### **The Stabilisation Engine**

Variation, encounter, and mediation operating on an existing closure to convert transient resolution into durable regime. The ribosome is the first biological anchor: ribosomal self-production is a closure in which the translational apparatus regenerates the components of its own continuation \- variation arriving as thermal fluctuation and transcriptional noise, encounter as codon-anticodon binding, mediation as the elongation cycle that propagates constraint through the nascent peptide. The adaptive immune system is the second: a transient antigen-encounter is stabilised into a durable clonal population of memory lymphocytes maintained at measurable metabolic cost \- variation arriving as somatic hypermutation, encounter as antigen binding, mediation as the selection cycle that propagates the high-affinity clone through successive rounds of proliferation.[^45] Contrast: the Bénard convection cell achieves spatial order through dissipation but does not regenerate its own boundary conditions \- sever the heat gradient and the pattern vanishes without a regime-specific cessation signature. Pathology: transient fluctuation \- a resolution that fires once and dissipates before the loop closes produces nothing durable.
