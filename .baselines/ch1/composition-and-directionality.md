### **Composition and Directionality**

The three engines compose in a dependency sequence: genesis produces what stabilisation consolidates, consolidation produces the closure from which stratification seeds the next field \- but the ordering is an empirical generalisation, not a logical necessity. The directionality is retrospective \- legible from within an already-stratified world \- not prospective. No engine "aims at" the next register. The engines compose; they do not conspire.[^48]

The engines' logic does not terminate at the biological register. At every register above brute persistence, the same structural audit applies \- what is maintained, in what currency, at what rate, and what fails when maintenance stops? The institutional register, where the maintenance currency shifts from ATP to person-hours and enforcement budgets, extends the engine vocabulary to a domain the framework can name but cannot yet price at the highest epistemic tier (1.2, Epistemic Tiers).[^49]
