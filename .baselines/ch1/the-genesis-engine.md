### **The Genesis Engine**

Variation, encounter, and mediation operating on a metastable field to precipitate a new individual with non-constructible control parameters. The crystallisation from 1.2 is the canonical below-fold case; the planarian's chemotactic coupling (1.1) is genesis across the fold \- the same three functions, but mediation closes into a self-referential loop funded by continuous expenditure. Contrast: thermal noise in a system at equilibrium \- variation without metastable tension \- produces fluctuations that dissipate without crossing any barrier.[^44] Pathology: sterile metastability \- variation without encounter yields nothing; a supersaturated solution that is never seeded remains indefinitely deferred.
