## **1.4 The Fold**

The three-function composition specified in 1.2 \- variation, encounter, mediation \- is a compositional structure: three irreducible functional roles whose simultaneous instantiation is the minimum physics enforces on any durably persisting creative regime. The *engines* are that composition's modal firings, differentiated by the target on which the composition operates. Operate on a metastable field and the composition fires as *genesis*; operate on an existing closure and it fires as *stabilisation*; operate on the products of interacting closures and it fires as *stratification*.[^38]

But before the engines, the fold.

The crystal and the worm exhibit identical compositional logic. Variation, encounter, and mediation fire in both. Yet they occupy categorically different persistence conditions. The crystal's lattice is a *paid-up fundamental*: installed at a one-time thermodynamic toll (the crystallisation enthalpy released as the system descends toward its energetic minimum), persisting without further expenditure because its bonding architecture is energetically favoured. The worm's constraint-architecture is a *rent-paying structure*: maintained at continuous burn rate by expenditure driven against the thermodynamic gradient, defaulting to dissolution the moment throughput stops. One persistence condition cannot be reduced to the other, because the rent-paying structure's existence is constitutively processual \- throughput-dependent, cessation-indexed, continuously re-earned \- while the paid-up fundamental's existence is constitutively structural. The crystal pays downhill; the cell pays uphill.[^39]

The fold is categorical in outcome but continuous in mechanism \- not a logical binary but a physical threshold, a critical point at which persistence conditions shift from energetic favourability to throughput-dependence. Below the fold, structure persists because dismantling it costs more than leaving it in place. Above the fold, structure persists only because a self-referential loop continuously pays the rent. The transition is a sharp threshold: the order parameter (self-referential closure, operationalised as positive burn rate indexed by cessation signature) is either zero or positive \- systems either self-maintain or they do not \- and the shift between regimes is discontinuous in outcome even when the underlying mechanism (variation, encounter, mediation) is continuous in kind. The formal apparatus for a rigorous thermodynamic limit \- the free-energy functional and the scaling argument \- is among Chapter 2's obligations.[^40]

Borderline cases sharpen the fold rather than blurring it. Viruses propagate \- they commandeer a host cell's translational machinery to replicate their genomes \- but they do not self-maintain: sever the host and the virion is inert, with no cessation signature of its own, because there is no self-referential closure whose throughput could stop. Prions propagate conformational change through a protein population but satisfy none of the triad's three functions: no variation from a metastable field, no encounter in the relevant sense, no mediating constraint \- only templated conversion. Autocatalytic micelles (cf. 1.2, fn \[^27\]) replicate their boundary structure but lack the self-referential return that constitutes closure: the micelle's products do not govern the conditions of the micelle's own continuation. All three are below the fold or sub-triadic \- and their exclusion is diagnostic, not stipulative: the cessation-test, applied to each, returns null.[^41]

The fold is where a self-maintaining system first has something at stake, because crossing back below the fold means ceasing to exist as the regime one is. What Jonas (1966) characterised as the organism's needful self-concern \- the stake in its own continuation \- compositional immanence locates in entropy production: the cessation signature is the physical structure whose interruption constitutes the loss Jonas described phenomenologically.[^42] The phenomenological treatment is deferred; what matters here is the structural correspondence.

The fold also locates the precise divergence from the Free Energy Principle. The correspondences are genuine: Markov blanket maps onto closure boundary, active inference maps onto burn rate, surprise-minimisation maps onto purchased absence.[^43] But the FEP presupposes the boundary that compositional immanence derives. The Markov blanket is given as the starting condition for inference; the fold specifies the thermodynamic conditions under which such a boundary comes to exist and requires continuous maintenance. The cessation-test, the burn-rate metric, and the fold together block the inflationary slide from "has a Markov blanket" (every system with statistically separable internal and external states) to "is an agent" (a system whose continued existence requires priced self-maintenance). The fold is the discriminator the FEP lacks: below it, blankets are free; above it, blankets cost.

### **The Genesis Engine**

Variation, encounter, and mediation operating on a metastable field to precipitate a new individual with non-constructible control parameters. The crystallisation from 1.2 is the canonical below-fold case; the planarian's chemotactic coupling (1.1) is genesis across the fold \- the same three functions, but mediation closes into a self-referential loop funded by continuous expenditure. Contrast: thermal noise in a system at equilibrium \- variation without metastable tension \- produces fluctuations that dissipate without crossing any barrier.[^44] Pathology: sterile metastability \- variation without encounter yields nothing; a supersaturated solution that is never seeded remains indefinitely deferred.

### **The Stabilisation Engine**

Variation, encounter, and mediation operating on an existing closure to convert transient resolution into durable regime. The ribosome is the first biological anchor: ribosomal self-production is a closure in which the translational apparatus regenerates the components of its own continuation \- variation arriving as thermal fluctuation and transcriptional noise, encounter as codon-anticodon binding, mediation as the elongation cycle that propagates constraint through the nascent peptide. The adaptive immune system is the second: a transient antigen-encounter is stabilised into a durable clonal population of memory lymphocytes maintained at measurable metabolic cost \- variation arriving as somatic hypermutation, encounter as antigen binding, mediation as the selection cycle that propagates the high-affinity clone through successive rounds of proliferation.[^45] Contrast: the Bénard convection cell achieves spatial order through dissipation but does not regenerate its own boundary conditions \- sever the heat gradient and the pattern vanishes without a regime-specific cessation signature. Pathology: transient fluctuation \- a resolution that fires once and dissipates before the loop closes produces nothing durable.

### **The Stratification Engine**

Variation, encounter, and mediation operating on products of interacting closures to seed a new register whose macrovariables are non-constructible from those of the constituent closures. Schematically: in a prebiotic scenario, interacting autocatalytic cycles (each a closure) generate products whose combinatorial interactions open a new metastable field \- a field of chemical possibilities unavailable to any single cycle, from which a higher-order closure can precipitate.[^46] The scenario is schematic, not historical; no specific origin-of-life model is endorsed. The crystal is the purest counter-case: register-locked, indefinitely durable, endogenously sterile. An ant colony that elaborates caste structure without seeding a new register illustrates register-locked complexity \- its macrovariables remain constructible from the individual organisms' behavioural repertoire.[^47] Pathology: closures elaborate without seeding a new register, producing baroque internal differentiation that never crosses the threshold into a categorially new level.

### **Composition and Directionality**

The three engines compose in a dependency sequence: genesis produces what stabilisation consolidates, consolidation produces the closure from which stratification seeds the next field \- but the ordering is an empirical generalisation, not a logical necessity. The directionality is retrospective \- legible from within an already-stratified world \- not prospective. No engine "aims at" the next register. The engines compose; they do not conspire.[^48]

The engines' logic does not terminate at the biological register. At every register above brute persistence, the same structural audit applies \- what is maintained, in what currency, at what rate, and what fails when maintenance stops? The institutional register, where the maintenance currency shifts from ATP to person-hours and enforcement budgets, extends the engine vocabulary to a domain the framework can name but cannot yet price at the highest epistemic tier (1.2, Epistemic Tiers).[^49]
