### **What Remains Unformalised**

The vocabulary is installed but not yet derived. The Landauer floor has been invoked throughout as a thermodynamic lower bound \- the minimum cost of discrimination \- but the formal derivation that converts it from a cited result into the framework's foundational audit tool has been deferred. The Stratogonic Principle \- the criterion for when a new register is genuine rather than a convenient redescription \- has been named and its necessity exhibited at the beaker and the worm, but its formal statement awaits the quantitative apparatus. The non-constructibility argument's conjunction has been stated, but the formal proof that the conjunction closes both escape routes requires the derivation the next chapter will supply. Until that derivation is in hand, the vocabulary remains a promissory note backed by physical intuition \- powerful intuition, grounded in SI-measurable quantities at the cellular register, but intuition nonetheless. The framework's own epistemic-tier discipline (1.2) requires the admission.[^63]
