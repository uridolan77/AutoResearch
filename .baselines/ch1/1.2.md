## **1.2 Compositional Immanence**

A supersaturated sodium acetate solution sits at room temperature \- dissolved ions carrying a crystalline future not yet discharged, incompatible potentials held in thermodynamic deferral. The system occupies a metastable minimum in a free-energy landscape, where the order parameter (degree of crystalline ordering) is dynamically inert: no macroscopic descriptor in the solution's adequate state-variable set \- temperature, concentration, pressure \- corresponds to the lattice's bonding angles and periodicities. Touch a seed crystal to the surface. Within seconds a lattice propagates outward, the solution's excess enthalpy discharging as heat you can feel through the glass \- approximately 265 kJ/kg, the thermodynamic trace of installation.[^10]

Three functions compose in this event, and none is dispensable.

*Variation*: the dissolved ions occupy a range of energetically accessible configurations, none yet selected \- a superabundance of incompatible potentials held in metastable deferral. The field is tense with unresolved difference, but no difference has been resolved. Simondon called this the *préindividuel* \- the metastable field of incompatible potentials ontologically prior to any individual.[^11] Two commitments from Simondon's framework are structurally necessary here and are retained throughout: metastability is ontologically prior (you do not start with individuals; you start with fields of unresolved potentials), and individuation is an operation, not a selection among pre-given options (the landscape is installed, not consulted).[^12] The supersaturated solution is a thermodynamic préindividuel, carrying potentials whose resolution will precipitate an individual the field's own vocabulary cannot name.

*Encounter*: the seed crystal is the dyadic event that carries the field across the nucleation barrier ΔG∗ \- the free-energy toll of installation. Brute, contingent, local: this crystal, this surface, this instant. It could have failed to occur. The encounter's contingency is *which* resolution occurs \- this seed, this surface, this instant \- not *whether* resolution occurs. Statistical mechanics predicts that metastable states resolve; the question is not if but when and where. *Mediation*: the governance the seed initiated propagates through the lattice. Each newly incorporated ion constrains the position and orientation of the next, and the coordination that no single ion performs is the irreducible third function \- global order emerging from local constraint-propagation. Mediation is governance paid for in dissipation: the crystallisation enthalpy is the thermodynamic receipt. The crystallisation is irreversible: the supersaturated state is consumed, not preserved alongside the lattice.[^13]

The crystal pays \- but the crystallisation enthalpy is a one-time phase-transition toll, thermodynamically distinct from the continuous entropy production that funds a living closure.[^14] Once the lattice has formed, it persists because its bonding architecture is energetically favoured \- no self-referential loop regenerates the conditions; the conditions hold. *The crystal precipitates; the worm purchases.* The planarian's chemotactic coupling (1.1) maps onto the same triad \- gradient as variation, binding event as encounter, closure-regeneration loop as mediation \- but mediation closes into a self-referential loop funded by continuous ATP expenditure. The crystal pays the toll of individuation but not the rent of persistence. The worm pays both. The commitment to ongoing maintenance is what introduces closure \- and with closure, the possibility of regime-specific death. Mediation is defined throughout by two necessary conditions: constraint-propagation across a domain the encounter opened, and governance of that domain's subsequent dynamics. Biological closure is a *specification* of mediation \- adding self-referential return \- not a different function.

### **Non-Constructibility**

The lattice's bonding angles and periodicities were not actual properties of the dissolved state \- they became available only when the crystallisation event inaugurated a regime whose dynamical vocabulary the prior regime could not name. The order parameter ξ is dynamically inert in the metastable basin and becomes the dominant descriptor in the crystalline basin. Those descriptors are not hidden in the solution, awaiting discovery. They become structurally available only when the phase transition installs the regime they describe.[^15]

When this book calls such macrovariables *non-constructible*, the claim is operational: under finite memory, resolution, and dissipation budgets, no physically embedded agent can recover a new regime's dominant control parameters from the prior regime's state-variable set as a practicable reconstruction strategy. Two independent considerations motivate this: any subsystem attempting exhaustive microstate reconstruction would saturate its own thermal environment with the waste heat of its discriminations, and there is evidence that for certain systems, macro-descriptions carry greater effective information (in Hoel's technical sense) than micro-descriptions \- more deterministic, less degenerate causal structure \- even though the macro supervenes entirely on the micro.[^16] The conjunction of thermodynamic self-defeat and informational inferiority is what renders "in principle constructible" physically vacuous. The formal argument that closes both escape routes requires the derivation Ch. 2 will supply; what matters in this chapter is the operational commitment and its motivation.[^17]

A clarification is needed here, because "discrimination" has been doing varied work. Three senses are in play: *biological discrimination* (the ion pump selects this ion, not that), *measurement discrimination* (the instrument resolves this state from that), and *logical discrimination* (the computation erases this bit). What transfers across all three is the finite-resolution, finite-memory, finite-dissipation structure \- every discrimination in every sense requires a physical system to maintain at least one distinction at non-zero cost. What does not transfer is semantic content: the ion pump does not "mean" anything by its selectivity, and the claim that discrimination is priced carries no commitment about representation.[^18]

### **Closure and Purchased Absence**

The worm's chemotactic coupling, exhibited in 1.1, is a *closure*: a dynamical regime in which the system's own dissipative expenditure regenerates the boundary conditions that channel further expenditure, indexed by a cessation signature. The ion pumps regenerate the membrane gradient that channels the ion flow that funds the ion pumps. Each pass through the loop reproduces the boundary conditions that enabled the previous pass. Montévil and Mossio's (2015) framework treats organisms as networks of *closure of constraints* \- constraints that mutually depend on and maintain the processes they constrain \- and this is the closest published peer to compositional immanence's closure concept.[^19] Montévil and Mossio's framework tells you *that* closure obtains; compositional immanence tells you *what it costs* and *what happens when the cost is no longer met*.

What the worm holds at bay is an equilibrium \- the state in which every gradient has discharged and every distinction has dissolved. Every act of closure-regeneration is an expenditure on behalf of what is *not there*: death deferred, dissolution prevented, the equilibrium that would erase the organism's boundary kept from arriving. This is *purchased absence* \- and the term does the work of several: it names the structure that makes closure more than feedback, maintenance more than throughput, and persistence more than inertia. The closure's norms are not imposed from outside; they are constituted by the closure's self-referential structure \- what counts as failure is determined by what the closure must do to continue.

Dissipation alone does not constitute purchased absence. A candle flame dissipates energy and exhibits self-organising form \- but its combustion consumes the wick, destroying the conditions of its own persistence. A cell regenerates those conditions. The flame spends and dies. The cell spends and continues. The BZ reaction rides an externally supplied gradient rather than closing a self-regenerative loop \- it fades without revealing a self-regenerating constraint-architecture, because it has none.[^20] The burn-rate metric operationalises what Deacon's qualitative morphodynamic/teleodynamic hierarchy correctly identifies: the categorical difference between self-organising form and self-regenerating closure. The cessation-test (below) supplies the quantitative criterion Deacon's framework identifies as necessary but does not provide.[^21]

### **The Constraint-Architecture**

A constraint-architecture seals boundaries \- the cell membrane's lipid bilayer holding distinct chemical environments apart, the vesicle wall concentrating reactants against diffusion. It steers flows \- ion channels directing Na⁺ and K⁺ along electrochemical gradients, enzymatic cascades routing metabolic intermediates to specific reaction sites. It repairs damage \- receptor proteins resynthesised after binding events, cytoskeletal filaments rebuilt after mechanical stress, membrane integrity restored after perturbation. Sealing, steering, repairing: each is a thermodynamic operation with a measurable cost. The burn rate is the aggregate price of these three operations, running continuously, and it is what the cessation signature enumerates when the funding stops \- the cessation-test (below) operationalises this enumeration.

### **The Triad's Irreducibility**

The irreducibility is exhibited at the beaker. Remove variation and there is no field tension to resolve \- the solution has no excess enthalpy to discharge. Remove encounter and the field remains unresolved \- a supersaturated solution without a seed crystal. Remove mediation and the encounter produces a transient response that dissipates without governance \- the nucleation event fires but no lattice propagates. Sub-triadic configurations are dynamically unstable: in each case, the configuration either equilibrates or installs the missing function and crosses into full composition. Reduce to two and you get either a feedback loop without substrate regeneration (encounter \+ mediation without variation: the system runs but cannot adapt) or substrate variation without governance (variation \+ encounter without mediation: the system fluctuates but never closes). Neither persists under perturbation.[^22] Peirce's Reduction Thesis provides independent formal motivation: triadic relations cannot be decomposed into any combination of monadic and dyadic relations \- a convergence with the thermodynamic argument that is striking but not deductive.[^23]

### **The Cessation-Test**

The priced-operation concept, defined in the Thesis and Method bridge (1.1), anchors everything else in this section's vocabulary \- burn rate, cessation signature, unpriced operation are downstream articulations. The quantitative floor beneath the definition receives its formal derivation in the subsequent chapter.

Not every throughput-dependent system instantiates the triad. A waterfall, a fire, and a powered computer all cease when throughput is severed \- but none regenerates its own throughput conditions. The cessation-test distinguishes closure from mere dependence by enforcing three conditions jointly:

- *Self-referential closure*: the system's own expenditure regenerates the boundary conditions that channel further expenditure. The waterfall fails \- its throughput does not regenerate the gorge that channels it. Without this condition, mere throughput-dependence would count as closure.  
- *Categorically distinct cessation*: the collapse is categorically different from adjacent regimes, not merely quantitatively diminished \- organic molecules trending toward equilibrium after cell death are not "slower metabolism" but the loss of a self-maintaining architecture. Without this condition, gradual degradation would be indistinguishable from regime failure.  
- *Novel macrovariable vocabulary*: the system has generated macrovariables non-constructible from the prior register's vocabulary \- "membrane potential" refers to a relational architecture whose existence *is* the closure, not to a summation of bond energies. Without this condition, any self-maintaining system (a thermostat) would qualify as a closure.[^24]

The three conditions are jointly necessary. The conditions are individually insufficient because each, on its own, admits systems the framework must exclude.

A conceptual protocol for applying the test: hold the system's environment fixed (nutrient supply, temperature, pressure); vary throughput by controlled reduction of the system's energy source; monitor macrovariables identified by the system's own dynamics (membrane potential, metabolic flux, structural integrity); record the order and rate of macrovariable collapse. The cessation signature is the observed sequence. The protocol does not presuppose a system decomposition \- it discovers one by tracking which macrovariables degrade first, and the regime-specific order of degradation is the evidence that the macrovariable set is the system's own, not the observer's projection.[^25]

The cessation-test is not circular. A worked example: particular protein-folding intermediates \- molten-globule states maintained at measurable metabolic cost \- turn out to be priced-maintenance structures in their own right, revealed only when their cessation signature exposed hierarchical sub-closures within the larger cellular closure. The regime-structure was not antecedently posited; it was uncovered by applying the test.[^26] Independent support comes from Ladyman and Ross's "real pattern" criterion: a pattern is real if it carries information that would be lost under any redescription that eliminates it \- and the cessation signature is precisely such information.[^27]

A *creative regime* satisfies two criteria jointly: endogenous installation \- the capacity to install new constraint-identities from within \- and cross-cycle inheritance \- the capacity to stabilise those installations across cycles. The diamond lattice is the purest conservative persistence: sub-triadic, indefinitely durable, endogenously sterile. The instability claim is testable: self-replicating micelles exhibit encounter and variation but lack constraint-closure \- they replicate without self-maintaining, and their empirical fragility confirms the sub-triadic diagnosis.[^28]

### **Epistemic Tiers**

The Thesis and Method bridge (1.1) introduced the gradient of epistemic confidence as a recurring signpost. Here is the full articulation. Not all of this framework's claims carry the same epistemic weight:

- At the **cellular register**, burn rate is SI-measurable and the cessation-test produces unambiguous signatures; failure to produce a reproducible cessation signature falsifies the framework at its strongest point.  
- At the **neural register**, metabolic proxies (glucose uptake, oxygen consumption) operationalise the burn rate; absence of systematic correlation would defeat the proxy claim.  
- At the **institutional register**, the maintenance currency \- person-hours, enforcement budgets, legitimation throughput \- is constituted by the very closure-types the lower registers individuate; this circularity, while not vicious, is the framework's most exposed commitment.[^29]

The institutional register is a structural homology claim whose evidential status is separate from the thermodynamic claims at the cellular and neural registers. These claims remain programmatic until a worked case discharges the obligation \- and no argument in this chapter depends on them. Any assertion that cannot be located on this gradient of epistemic confidence has not yet earned its generality.
