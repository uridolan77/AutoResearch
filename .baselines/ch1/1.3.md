## **1.3 The Rival Audit**

The seventeenth-century settlement split reality along a joint that does not track the phenomenon \- *res extensa* gave mechanics its predictive grip, *res cogitans* gave the experiencing subject a domicile, and no verb for maintenance at a price survived the cut.[^30] Two repair programmes rise on its compromised credit, each correctly identifying a post-bifurcation pressure, each leaving a constitutive operation without a cessation-test.

The audit is immanent \- performed by systems with budgets, not from a transcendent standpoint. Any philosophical framework operates on three layers: logical validity, metaphysical interpretation, and physical coherence \- whether the standpoint the interpretation assumes can be occupied by a physically possible system. The audit targets only the third layer. It does not contest the mathematics or the phenomenology. It asks whether a framework's constitutive operations can survive the cessation-test: whether the framework can say what happens when the operation it invokes stops being funded.[^31]

The question in every case is not whether the framework fails \- each identifies something real \- but what it cannot diagnose about its own key operation.

### **Object-Oriented Ontology**

The version under audit is the one in which the real object is constitutively foreclosed from all relation \- Harman's defining thesis.[^32]

Harman begins with a correct insight: no interaction exhausts what an entity is. The hammer exceeds this moment of hammering; the organism exceeds this moment of measurement. Reduction to current relational profile is a falsification of experience. Compositional immanence inherits the insight without reservation: relational inexhaustibility is non-negotiable.

The move from relational inexhaustibility to constitutive withdrawal \- the thesis that the real object is sealed from all relational contact \- requires a distinction (withdrawn/sensual) maintained across every encounter and every context. A distinction maintained by energetic favourability \- the diamond's lattice \- persists without throughput, but only against perturbations below its stability threshold. Constitutive withdrawal claims the stronger condition: stability against all relational contact. That condition requires active regeneration under arbitrary perturbation, which is a throughput-dependent operation \- yet the framework specifies no throughput and provides no cessation-test for the withdrawn/sensual boundary.[^33]

The consequence falls at genesis: if real objects are constitutively sealed from relational installation, no encounter can precipitate a new constraint-identity. Vicarious causation rearranges sensual profiles; it cannot install the closure that would constitute a new regime. The genesis problem (1.1) has no purchase in a framework whose real objects are inaccessible to the relational structures from which closures precipitate.

Compositional immanence relocates the surplus Harman correctly identifies: it is ahead of the object, in the metastable field's undischarged potentials, not behind it in concealment. The marble block exceeds the sculptor's chisel not because a withdrawn core hides from relation, but because the block's crystalline microstructure carries thermodynamic potentials \- fracture planes, stress distributions, grain boundaries \- that no single encounter can discharge. Inexhaustibility is funded by metastable surplus, not by ontological seclusion.

### **New Materialism**

The version under audit is the one that distributes agency flatly across all matter without discriminating constraint-types \- Bennett's defining move.[^34]

Barad's agential realism, the camp's strongest version, is engaged as a structural ally rather than a target: her "agential cuts" are explicitly enacted rather than given, and the production of boundaries has material conditions.[^35]

Barad's apparatus concept is the nearest published peer to compositional immanence's thesis that boundaries are material achievements with material conditions \- a structural convergence deeper than terminological overlap. In this respect, Barad and compositional immanence share a commitment that Bennett's version does not operationalise: boundaries are achievements, not givens.

Bennett correctly insists that matter is not inert. Thing-power names something real: the power grid acts, the microbiome shapes its host, the river carves its valley. The recovery of an agential dimension the bifurcation had legislated out of existence is necessary, and compositional immanence does not retreat from it.

The diagnostic absence is burn-rate discrimination. The power grid regenerates its own operating conditions; the candle flame consumes its wick; the river erodes without self-maintenance. Thing-power can say all three act, but it cannot say the first is categorically different from the third \- that one regenerates the conditions of its own dissipation while the other merely dissipates. Without the cessation-test, the morphodynamic/teleodynamic cut (1.2) is invisible: the framework cannot distinguish what happens when each system's throughput stops. The divergence with Barad sharpens at durability: her framework can say that a cut is enacted and that different apparatuses produce different cuts, but it cannot say what it costs to sustain one cut rather than another over time \- and it is that cost differential, legible in the cessation signature, that separates the transient from the self-regenerating.

The cessation-test discriminates: the power grid's cessation signature reveals a closure that regenerates its own throughput conditions. The river has no such signature; its erosion consumes without self-maintaining. Bennett's insight that all three are active survives. The cessation-test discriminates *what kind* of activity each sustains \- one-time toll versus continuous rent.

### **Closing Diagnostic**

In each case \- and in speculative realism's stability-as-default, which carries the same diagnostic signature \- the framework identifies a real pressure the bifurcation left unresolved, then resolves it through an operation whose cessation conditions are never specified.[^36] The same diagnostic signature appears in speculative realism's hyper-chaos: if regularities may vanish without reason, the geological record that the arche-fossil argument requires becomes unreliable testimony \- the cessation conditions of stability itself are unspecified.

What recurs is a single structural pattern: an operation that requires discrimination, maintenance, or traversal is performed without the framework being able to say what happens when the operation stops. The audit is not a dismissal. Each framework's insight survives into compositional immanence's vocabulary, relocated from a standpoint that lacks a cessation-test to one that has it.[^37]
