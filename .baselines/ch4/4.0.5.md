## **4.0.5 Debts Declared by This Chapter**

Three debts are opened here and remain unpaid at chapter's close:

1. The system-environment partition (flagged §4.1.1). Closure operates within a regime where the system-environment split is already operationally valid. The beaker's walls are presupposed, not derived. Ch. 6 will extend the lattice to the cosmological scale, where the partition itself is the product of prior transductive operations with non-zero burn rates. Debt \- unpaid. Assigned to Ch. 6\.  
2. Engine specifications. The three engines \- genesis (ontogenesis), materialisation (hylogenesis), stratification (normogenesis) \- are posed as problems in §4.6 but not resolved. Each requires domain-specific ordeal: biological morphogenesis (Ch. 5), cosmological constitution (Ch. 6), normative stratification (Ch. 7). Debt \- unpaid. Assigned to Chs. 5-7.  
3. Cognitive and symbolic burn-rate operationalisation. The triadic grammar is demonstrated at the molecular scale. Its extension to regimes where constraint-architecture is maintained by semiotic, cognitive, and institutional processes \- where "burn rate" must be operationalised as metabolic cost of neural computation, institutional maintenance expenditure, and cultural reproduction overhead \- is programmatic. Debt \- unpaid. Assigned to Chs. 8-10.
