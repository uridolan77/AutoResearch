## **4.2.4 Will as Primitive**

Despite Deleuze's efforts to deflate the concept into relational force-differentials, a residue persists. If the will to power is truly nothing more than the differential internal to force-relations, then its work is done by the differential itself, and "will" is a redundant metaphysical supplement. But if the will retains an irreducible surplus \- an intensity behind the differential, a primitive drive prior to the forces it differentiates \- then a metaphysical subject has been reinstalled at the base of nature. This is Nietzsche's Ariadne thread: the Will as ontological primitive.

The structural pathology is precise: the Will-as-primitive is the attempt to ground encounter in a hidden monadic intensity prior to all relation, from which the plurality of forces supposedly emanates. If the Will is truly prior to the forces it differentiates, then encounter is not constitutive but derivative, and structural 2 collapses back into structural 1 \- a single primitive ground masquerading as relational plurality. The same oscillation that plagued Axis I reappears inside the Nietzschean system: the Will threatens to become the substance behind the modes, the One behind the Many.

The deflationary resolution at the level of the beaker: the crystal does not will to crystallise \- the Gibbs free-energy differential determines the trajectory. The Will-as-primitive violates causal closure by positing a force behind forces that the sealed ledger cannot track; it violates energetic closure by introducing an intensive surplus that cannot be cashed out as burn rate; and it violates explanatory closure by grounding the observable differential in an unobservable metaphysical subject.

The metabolisation is not completed here; it is distributed across the four operators that follow. Bergson converts the Will's temporal drive into the thermodynamic arrow; Whitehead converts the command of force into structured inheritance; Simondon converts agonistic encounter into propagative physical contact; Deleuze rewrites the agonistic field as a structured intensive landscape with no primitive will behind the gradient. By the time the lattice is complete, every function the Will-as-primitive was invoked to perform will have been redistributed across immanent operators, and the "will" that remains is not a metaphysical subject but a compressed descriptor for the field's own differential activity.
