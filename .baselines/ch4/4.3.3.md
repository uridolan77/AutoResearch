## **4.3.3 The Pivot**

Duration secures the arrow \- irreversible, cosmic, constitutive \- but it bequeaths a specific deficit: continuous flux without joints. If reality is pure *durée* \- unbroken heterogeneous qualitative flow \- the schema lacks an account of how discrete entities precipitate from that flow. The crystal is not a slice of flux; it is a bounded structure with an inside and an outside, a lattice geometry and a surface, that constrains subsequent events in ways the surrounding liquid does not.

Bergson's duration achieves temporal mediation \- irreversible accumulation that seals expenditure \- but it underarticulates the architectural mediation through which governance achieves determinate structure. Continuous qualitative flow is mediation without discretisation; it accounts for why the products of encounter cannot un-happen (the arrow) but not for how the products of encounter achieve bounded, countable, inheritable form (the occasion). What is still missing is the unit of composition \- the discrete event in which variation is prehended, encounter is resolved, and mediation crystallises into a determinate datum available for inheritance. Whitehead provides it.
