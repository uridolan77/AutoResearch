## **4.2.5 The Deficit**

The crisis engine is installed and the 2→3 cycle operative \- encounter destabilises, sedimentation re-stabilises, and the burn rate is positive: every regularity carries a thermodynamic receipt, every crisis spends real gradients. But the engine remains temporally agnostic: nothing in the Nietzschean framework alone prevents the products of encounter from un-happening, the dissolved state from overpowering the crystalline configuration in a reversal that leaves no trace. The ledger is open in both directions \- each payment can be refunded, each expenditure unwound \- and without a seal on expenditure itself, mediation is not truly purchased but merely borrowed. Bergson supplies the temporal seal.
