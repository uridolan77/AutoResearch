## **4.6.2 Hylogenesis: The Stabilisation Engine**

The problem. How are the material regimes within which individuation operates themselves constituted? The coupling constants, bonding affinities, and thermodynamic gradients that the beaker presupposes were themselves installed by prior individuations at earlier energy scales. Every regularity the genealogy has treated as a constraint-floor \- the electromagnetic interactions governing molecular bonding, the symmetry structures defining lattice possibilities, the activation barriers determining nucleation thresholds \- is, once the Peircean conversion is taken seriously, the sedimented output of transductive operations whose burn rates dwarf anything the laboratory can reproduce. Ontogenesis asks how individuals precipitate within a regime; hylogenesis asks how the regime itself was laid down.

The operators. Hylogenesis extends two operators to their cosmological limit. Peirce's evolutionary constraint \- the principle that every regularity is a sedimented habit carrying a thermodynamic receipt \- supplies the fundamental logic: laws have histories, and their histories have costs. The burn-rate diagnostic operationalises this logic at each stratum: identify the regularity, locate its thermodynamic receipt, demonstrate that its persistence requires either ongoing expenditure (actively maintained constraint \- regime-regenerative dissipation above the dissolution horizon) or sufficient depth of sedimentation (passively robust constraint whose perturbation would require energy inputs exceeding the system's available fluctuations).

Two sub-operations formalise the passage from fragile product to durable regime:

* The Witness \- redundant inscription \- names the process by which multiple independent instantiations of the same constraint-pattern make the pattern robust against local perturbation. Every unit cell replicating the same bonding geometry is a Witness to the lattice's constraint-architecture; every gauge boson mediating the same coupling is a Witness to the electroweak sedimentation. Robustness scales with redundancy: the more independent instantiations, the higher the perturbation threshold required to unseat the habit.  
* The Canon \- selective compression \- names the filtering process by which some sedimented habits achieve near-universal scope while others remain local and fragile. The specific polymorphic identity \- monoclinic rather than orthorhombic \- that the system's expenditure-history selected is the Canon: the compressed signature of which basin captured the system under these boundary conditions. At the cosmological scale, the Standard Model's coupling constants are ultra-deep Canons \- habits so thoroughly sedimented that they function as structural constants for all subsequent individuation, their perturbation requiring energies no longer accessible within the current cosmological regime.

The beaker correlate. The coupling constants governing sodium acetate's bonding geometry were deposited by electroweak symmetry-breaking. The scalar leap from beaker to Big Bang is licensed by the Peircean conversion: if every regularity is a sedimented habit, then the coupling constants are ultra-deep habits \- regularities whose thermodynamic structure is, in principle, of the same type as the habit-sedimentation visible at the crystal face, differing not in kind but in depth of sedimentation and magnitude of the burn rate that installed them. What the beaker calls its constraint-floor \- the bonding parameters, the lattice-symmetry groups, the activation barriers \- is a sedimentary column whose deepest layers were deposited at energy scales the laboratory cannot reproduce.

The debt. Hylogenesis is the commitment that the lattice is not merely a local grammar adequate to benchtop chemistry but a universal grammar of individuation whose scalar range extends, in principle, from gauge-coupling stabilisation to institutional constitution \- and that every extension must earn its keep under the same performance criteria (prediction, resource discipline, comparative testing) that governed the original extractions. The commitment is programmatic, and it binds the subsequent chapters to a specific sequence of ordeals. Chapter 6 addresses the constitution of the house itself \- the scope condition flagged in §4.1.2 \- by extending the lattice to the cosmological scale, where the system-environment partition that the beaker presupposed is itself the product of prior individuation: decoherence, symmetry-breaking, the installation of classical regimes from quantum substrates. At each scale, the ordeal is the same: identify the constraint-floor, demonstrate that it is the sedimented product of prior transductive operations with non-zero burn rates, and show that the operators assembled in this chapter \- not new operators imported ad hoc \- suffice to track the sedimentation.
