## **4.3.5 The Deficit**

Bergson sealed the ledger; Whitehead jointed the flux. Together they convert burn rate from positive-but-reversible to irreversibly positive and articulate continuous duration into discrete, inheritable occasions \- the arrow and the joint, the two structural preconditions Axis II could not supply. But each achieves Thirdness only by smuggling in a transcendent supplement: élan vital as the driver of creative novelty, Eternal Objects as the supplier of qualitative specificity, Creativity as the processual ground of composition. The pattern is now unmistakable \- six philosophers, six Ariadne threads, and the structural lesson sharpens with each iteration: wherever mediation is not explicitly secured and metabolically maintained, a transcendent supplement reappears to bear the load.

What the architecture still lacks is the mechanics of traversal \- how constituted structure propagates across the metastable domain through real physical contact \- and the cartography of the terrain \- why the front walks *this way* and not another. The occasion describes what happens at a single site; the arrow describes why the site's product cannot un-happen; but how a seed smaller than a grain of sand comes to organise the entire beaker remains open. Simondon supplies the mechanics; Deleuze supplies the map.
