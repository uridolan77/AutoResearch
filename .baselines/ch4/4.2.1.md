## **4.2.1 Evolutionary Constraint**

Peirce converts the eternal given into a historical product. The operator extracted is evolutionary constraint: the principle that the regularities governing individuation are themselves products of prior individuations \- sedimented habits rather than eternal axioms. Constraint shifts from an input of the schema, presupposed before the process begins, to an output generated by the process itself. Once installed, every Leibnizian law becomes legible as the limit case of a habit so thoroughly sedimented that it masquerades as logical entailment, and the burn rate ceases to be zero \- every regularity now carries a thermodynamic receipt.

Habit-taking is the genealogy's first immanently earned mediation: regularity is not pre-given but deposited through iterative history, and its persistence is not logically guaranteed but thermodynamically maintained. The burn rate becomes positive. Structural 3 \- the sedimentation of encounter into durable constraint \- enters the ledger. Stratogonic diagnosis: condition (i) met \- stabilised aspect-selection achieved through habit; condition (ii) met \- burn rate positive; condition (iii) partial \- constraint-closure approached but not sealed, because reversibility means the constraint-architecture is not yet closed against refund. At structural 1, Spinoza's substance absorbed all mediation into monism; at structural ∞, Leibniz outsourced it to a divine register at zero cost. Peirce's habit-taking earns mediation within the sealed field: the constraint-architecture that channels future individuations is itself a product of past individuations, and its continuation costs work.

"Habit" operates at two levels that must not be confused. The load-bearing deliverable is regime-relative stabilisation: the empirically confirmed claim that the regularities physicists treat as laws are parameters tied to symmetry structures at specific energy scales, and that developmental constraints in biology are likewise products of prior transitions. \[Operationalisable\]² Peirce's full cosmogonic hypothesis \- that the universe began in absolute indeterminacy and acquired all its regularities through habit-taking \- is speculative. The genealogy does not require it. Regime-relative stabilisation stands independently. But the cosmogonic image usefully dramatises the principle: laws have histories, and their histories have thermodynamic costs.

² At our best-tested level, the low-energy electromagnetic interaction appears as the unbroken remnant of the electroweak gauge structure after symmetry breaking; the Higgs discovery and precision electroweak tests support the reality of that mechanism, but they do not derive the numerical values of the couplings *ex nihilo*. In the Standard Model, those couplings function as measured parameters whose effective values run with scale \- the theory itself treated as an effective field theory tested up to TeV energies with an unknown cutoff. Regime-relative stabilisation is independently attested at confirmed energy scales and does not require cosmological speculation.

Sodium acetate crystallises as monoclinic prisms under standard conditions. Change the temperature gradient, introduce a different solvent, seed with a foreign crystal, and the same molecular species produces different polymorphs \- different lattice architectures governed by the same bonding affinities under different constraints. Polymorphism is the beaker's exhibition of regime-relative habit. \[Literal\] The bonding affinities that constitute the molecular species' operative rule do not dictate a single predetermined outcome but define a probability landscape \- a set of energetically accessible configurations whose specific resolution depends on local conditions at the moment of nucleation. If the *lex seriei* were an eternal decree, the same species under any conditions would yield the same crystal; the fact that it does not shows that the law is better read, once the habit-operator is installed, as the frozen image of a constraint that was itself deposited under specific boundary conditions.

The electromagnetic interactions governing molecular bonding are downstream consequences of the low-energy gauge structure; for the purposes of chemistry they function as effectively invariant parameters \- a regime-relative stabilisation at confirmed energy scales. Below that scale, the coupling constants operate as structural constants of every subsequent individuation. Polymorphism reveals that even within this ultra-rigid constraint-floor, the crystal's specific form remains underdetermined: the same bonding affinities yield monoclinic prisms or needle-like crystals depending on the local regime of temperature, pressure, and impurity concentration. Habit bends where the regime bends. The law does not break \- it expresses differently under different constraints, and each expression is a distinct individual whose defect signature records the contingent history of its own nucleation.

At the crystallisation front, habit-taking is visible at the molecular level. Each successfully incorporated molecule reinforces the constraint for the next \- not by transmitting an eternal law but by depositing a local regularity. The lattice geometry propagates because each layer presents a template that channels the bonding options of adjacent molecules, narrowing their probability distribution and increasing the likelihood that the next layer will replicate the same geometry. The regularity is the memory of the system \- a pattern inscribed through iterative history, not a rule consulted from outside.

But habit-taking, taken alone, describes only reinforcement: a probability funnel narrowing through repetition, each instantiation deepening the channel. It does not describe what happens when two incompatible constraint-regimes collide and one seizes the field. A universe of pure habit-taking would be a universe of monotonically increasing rigidity \- habits accumulating indefinitely, no habit ever challenged, no regime forced beyond its own limits. There is no account, within Peirce alone, of differential dominance \- why one habit overpowers another through encounter rather than peaceful coexistence.

This is the compositional limit of structural 3 without structural 2: mediation alone sediments but cannot destabilise. A universe that freezes by a different route than Spinoza's \- not through logical necessity but through monotonic reinforcement, the ratchet-click of habit hardening into pseudo-eternity. The triadic minimum demands that encounter remain active within the cycle, not merely presupposed as an initial condition. Peirce provides the sedimentation; what is missing is the engine of crisis.

Two Peircean residues are flagged.

*Agapic teleology*. In his 1893 "Evolutionary Love," Peirce posits *agape* \- cosmic love \- as the motor of increasing complexity: a creative attraction that draws the universe toward ever-richer coordination. The structural pathology is precise. Agapic teleology smuggles mediation back in at zero burn rate \- a Demon-class error violating Stratogonic condition (ii): a cosmic tendency toward coordination that costs nothing thermodynamically, that operates prior to and independently of the encounters it supposedly coordinates, and that performs the identical structural function as Leibniz's pre-established harmony at a cosmological scale. Where Leibniz's divine Understanding selected the best compossible world from the library of complete concepts, Peirce's *agape* draws the universe toward maximal coordination by sympathetic attraction. Both provide governance for free: coordination without expenditure, mediation without metabolic cost. The difference is cosmetic \- love replacing intellect \- while the compositional deficit is identical: mediation outsourced to a register the sealed ledger cannot audit. The crisis engine about to be installed already performs the work *agape* was invoked to do: encounter destabilises entrenched habits, and the differential between competing forces determines which regime prevails. What drives the universe toward new coordination is not sympathetic attraction but agonistic encounter under metastable tension \- and the thermodynamic cost of each coordination is non-zero and auditable.

*Strong tychism*. Peirce's ontological randomness \- absolute chance as an irreducible feature of reality prior to all habit \- is replaced by functional indeterminacy. A metastable system's energy configuration specifies a repertoire of admissible futures without selecting a unique one. Contingency is neither an occult spontaneity (variation promoted to ontological ground) nor a mere name for ignorance \- it is the structural fact that the nucleation barrier is low enough that thermal and concentration noise internal to the system can tip the balance, yet high enough that not every geometrically imaginable outcome is accessible. Strong tychism is refused; functional indeterminacy is retained.

Nietzsche installs the engine.
