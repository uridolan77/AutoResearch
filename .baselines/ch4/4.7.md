## **4.7 The Debt**

The crystal in the beaker has done its work. It was never an illustration. It was the ordeal that forced eight philosophical systems to surrender their transcendent safety-valves and rebuild their operators under immanent closure. What remains is a lattice \- not a history, not a ladder, but a crystallographic structure in which eight operators interlock as the minimal grammar of compositional immanence. Three engines have been posed as problems the lattice was built to articulate \- problems whose resolution will occupy the remainder of the book under the same constraint that built the lattice: no miracles, no tautologies, no free lunches in ontology. The sealed room is furnished, historicised, temporalised, discretised, propagated, and mapped. The operators have been installed \- but do they smuggle in the transcendence they were built to refuse?
