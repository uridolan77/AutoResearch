## **4.3.1 Bergson: Irreversible Duration**

Valid intuition. From Bergson's work a single compound operator is extracted \- irreversible duration \- assembled from the convergence of three operations: thermodynamic irreversibility, the critique of the retrospective illusion, and the cinematographic diagnosis. Each performs a distinct function, but their force falls as a unified intervention on the entire preceding stack. Duration is not the spatialised time of mechanics, where instants are laid out like points on a line and traversed by a mobile that remains self-identical throughout its journey; it is continuous, heterogeneous, qualitative flow in which each moment carries the whole of its past into itself. The schema does not require Bergson's phenomenological apparatus \- the introspective method by which he originally accessed *durée* through consciousness. It requires the structural consequence: irreversibility.

Irreversibility is what converts the 2→3 cycle from a reversible oscillation into a cumulative history. The Nietzsche-Peirce cycle \- encounter, overcoming, sedimentation, new metastability, new encounter \- was already operative, but nothing in the encounter-mediation coupling alone prohibited the cycle from running backward. Bergson's duration is exapted here as thermodynamic irreversibility \- the accumulation of entropy production in any real, dissipative process that prevents a system from returning to its initial state without external work. Prigogine's "order through fluctuation" independently anchors the same insight in physical chemistry: dissipative structures maintain their organisation precisely through irreversible entropy production, so that the thermodynamic arrow is not merely a statistical tendency but a constitutive condition of structured becoming. \[Operationalisable\] Peircean habit appears, in this key, as the cosmological sedimentation of such irreversible durations, while Spinoza's geometric necessity reads as the limit case in which this sedimentation has been idealised into timeless deduction. The Nietzschean extraction established that the burn rate is positive; Peirce had shown that every apparent law is a fossil of prior spending. Duration adds the temporal seal: the entropy produced by each transaction is dissipated into the environment and cannot be spontaneously recovered. Deacon's three-tiered dynamical hierarchy \- homeodynamic → morphodynamic → teleodynamic \- independently converges on the same structural demand: thermodynamic self-maintenance as the condition of genuine normativity, each tier constituted by the irreversible expenditure-history of the tier below, though without the genealogy's explicit triadic grammar or its method of exaptive extraction.

Bergson converts an open tab into a closed ledger \- each line item settled irreversibly, the running total monotonically increasing. Mediation is not merely thermodynamically purchased but thermodynamically irreversible \- each act of sedimentation costs real gradients whose expenditure cannot be undone, and the constraint-architecture that results is not a reversible configuration but a genuinely historical deposit. Burn rate acquires its full temporal meaning only here: not just positive (Peirce's gain over Axis I) but irreversibly positive \- each transaction permanently alters the field's boundary conditions, and the accumulating total constitutes the system's real history.

Duration is cosmic \- "the duration of the universe must therefore be one with the latitude of creation which can find place in it" \- and without this cosmological commitment the irreversibility operator applies only to conscious observation and leaves the physical process untouched. With it, the supersaturated solution's thermodynamic path-history is a real condition that inhabits the system's present state and determines its future possibilities. The past does not cease to exist; it preserves itself as an ontological stratum that is real without being actual \- the present evanescent, a knife-edge of becoming perpetually passing, the past ontologically substantial, a continuously accumulating virtual totality that contracts into each successive present as the condition of its determinacy. Bergson's virtual past anticipates the Deleuzian virtual field while also retroactively transforming Leibniz's complete concepts into snapshots taken from within an ongoing accumulation. \[Structural-analogical\]

Tested at the anchor. Watch the front advance. Heat dissipates. The supersaturated solution that has crystallised and been redissolved is not identical to one that has never crystallised \- its nucleation threshold is often lower, its crystal morphology on second solidification altered, with residual nuclei, surface microscratches, and redistributed impurities providing persistent sites that bias subsequent crystallisation. The re-dissolved solution's altered nucleation threshold is a durational fact: not a snapshot of a state but a qualitative modification of the field's own sensitivity, sedimented by prior expenditure and irreducible to any enumeration of before-and-after configurations. The system has aged. It carries in its present configuration the sedimented trace of prior expenditures \- a past that, in Bergson's formulation, "gnaws into the future and swells as it advances" \- not as a metaphorical resonance but as a calorimetrically measurable modification of the field's boundary conditions. \[Literal\] A reversible nucleation event \- one in which the system returned to its exact pre-crystallisation state without external energy input \- would refute the operator this section extracts. None has been observed.

Divergence. Two Ariadne threads must be named.

*First Ariadne thread: élan vital.* In *Creative Evolution*, Bergson posits an élan vital \- a current of creative energy coursing through evolution, branching and dissipating as it encounters the resistance of matter, driving life upward against entropic degradation. Closure forbids this import. If novelty requires a vital impulse injected from a source inaccessible to thermodynamic accounting, the sealed room has been punctured \- a vitalist skyhook smuggled past the immanence condition. The élan vital is a transcendent variation \- a monadic qualitative intensity (creative upward drive) that operates prior to and independently of the relational field it supposedly animates. It reproduces the structural logic of the Will-as-primitive flagged in Axis II: a hidden ground beneath the encounter, doing work the sealed ledger cannot audit. The Landauer Floor specifies the failure: the élan vital introduces a cosmic directionality toward increased organisation \- governance at zero burn rate \- that constitutes a Demon-class error (Ch. 2 §2.8.7), violating Stratogonic condition (ii). The schema replaces it with what the preceding imports already provide: metastability (Spinoza's closure under tension), generative law (Leibniz's operative individuality, secularised by Peirce into habit), habit-plasticity (Peirce's evolutionary operators), and agonistic encounter (Nietzsche's crisis engine). Novelty arises from the internal resources of a field whose incompatible potentials reach a threshold at which the current regime can no longer metabolise its own tensions \- not from a vital supplement the ledger cannot track.

*Second Ariadne thread: intuition.* Bergson argues that duration can only be grasped through intuition \- a direct, sympathetic participation in the flow of time that escapes the spatialising categories of the intellect. Intuition is a claim to privileged epistemic access outside the constraint-architecture \- a mode of knowing that bypasses the burn-rate economy of conceptual mediation, grasping its object at zero cost. Deleuze's resolution dissolves the impasse without conceding the premise: what he recovers from Bergson's own practice is the possibility of concepts adequate to their object precisely by tracking qualitative transformation and irreversible accumulation without spatialising them. Intuition is refused as a privileged epistemological standpoint; the durational concept \- a concept that moves with its object \- is retained as the schema's operative method.

Verdict. Convergence on irreversibility and the critique of the retrospective illusion; partial divergence on the élan vital and intuition. Bergson seals the ledger against refund \- each expenditure is constitutive and unrepeatable \- but punctures the sealed room with a vitalist skyhook that does work the beaker cannot audit.

Structural-number diagnosis. Assign σ \= 3‖, compromised. Stratogonic diagnosis: conditions (i)-(ii) met \- stabilised aspect-selection and non-zero burn rate secured through irreversibility; condition (iii) compromised \- constraint-closure requires supplementation by élan vital (governance at zero burn rate, Demon-class error). Duration provides the arrow; what it does not provide is the joint \- the discrete event-structure through which irreversible accumulation articulates itself into bounded, countable, inheritable units of composition.
