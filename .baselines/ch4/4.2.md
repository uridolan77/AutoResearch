## **4.2 Axis II \- Motor: History & Crisis | Peirce & Nietzsche**

Every law in the sealed room is eternal and every transaction pre-paid. The deficit is historical transformation. If the constraint-surface governing sodium acetate's polymorphic repertoire is treated as a timeless given \- bonding affinities inscribed in a divine understanding before any molecule ever encountered another \- then no genuine novelty has occurred; the crystal merely executes a programme whose complete concept was always already written.

The room needs a motor. Two operators install it. Nietzsche achieves structural 2 \- encounter in its purest philosophical articulation: agonistic collision of unequal forces whose differential determines which regime prevails and what qualitative character the result assumes. Stratogonic diagnosis: condition (i) met \- aspect-selection achieved through force-differential; condition (ii) met \- burn rate positive; condition (iii) fails \- no sedimentation mechanism to seal outcomes into constraint-closure. Peirce achieves structural 3 \- the first immanently earned mediation: the sedimentation of encounter into regularity, the conversion of contingent outcomes into durable constraints that govern subsequent individuations. The tension the axis stages: pure dyadic force cannot generate law, habit, or governance without mediation; sedimentation without a motor of encounter describes only monotonic reinforcement, never regime change. Neither operator alone completes the compositional minimum. Together they describe a cycle \- encounter, overcoming, sedimentation, new metastability, new encounter \- whose every turn is a thermodynamic transaction with non-zero cost. But together they also carry residues \- agapic teleology, strong tychism, the Will as ontological primitive \- that the remaining operators must metabolise.
