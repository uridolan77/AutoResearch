## **4.0.8 The Spiral**

The chapter's method is exaptation, not survey. Eight philosophers are structurally raided and each extraction is diagnosed by a structural number that records which of the three compositional functions the apparatus secures and which it outsources to a transcendent register \- a Demon-class error in the taxonomy of Ch. 2 §2.8.7. The spiral traverses four axes:

* Axis I \- House (Spinoza, Leibniz): the 1-∞ oscillation between undifferentiated closure and infinite individuality without encounter, both at burn rate zero. Stratogonic failure: none of the three conditions satisfied (no stabilised aspect-selection in a frozen plenum; zero burn rate; no constraint-closure without encounter to close over).  
* Axis II \- Motor (Peirce, Nietzsche): the 2→3 tension between agonistic encounter and habit-sedimentation, burn rate positive but each outcome still reversible. Stratogonic failure: condition (ii) partially met (burn rate positive) but condition (iii) \- constraint-closure \- not achieved (reversibility means the constraint-architecture is not sealed against refund).  
* Axis III \- Arrow (Bergson, Whitehead): the 3‖3 examination of temporal and architectural Thirdness, burn rate irreversible but residues \- élan vital, Eternal Objects, Creativity \- compromising the achievement. Stratogonic failure: conditions (i)-(ii) met, but condition (iii) \- constraint-closure \- compromised by transcendent supplements that provide governance at zero burn rate.  
* Axis IV \- Map & Act (Simondon, Deleuze): the 2‖3 movement from propagative mechanics to virtual cartography, culminating in the dissolution of dual-register immanence into stratified single-plane immanence. Stratogonic status: closest approach to full satisfaction; residues (Apeironic preindividual, transcendental virtual) identified and dissolved.

The spiral converges on three problems the rest of the book will solve \- ontogenesis, hylogenesis, normogenesis \- and the pattern it demonstrates is structural, not accidental: wherever Thirdness is not explicitly secured and metabolically maintained, a transcendent supplement reappears to bear the load. Every such supplement is a Demon-class error: it installs or maintains a distinction without entering it in the dissipative ledger.
