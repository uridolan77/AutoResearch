## **4.4.7 Allagmatics**

Allagmatics completes the apparatus as a general theory of operations that complements the sciences' theories of structure. Structures are always results of prior operations, and operations always presuppose structures they transform \- every concept must therefore be cashable as an explicit transformation of the metastable field, specifying which gradients it consumes, which compatibilities it produces, how it converts disparation into new regimes of stability \- or it is refused. The allagmatic demand retroactively disciplines every operator in the lattice: Spinoza's closure, Leibniz's *lex seriei*, Peirce's habit, Nietzsche's will to power, Bergson's duration, and Whitehead's concrescence must all show what they *do* to the field, not merely label its states. This is the anti-hylomorphic commitment in its most rigorous form: wherever form appears \- lattice geometry, cell morphology, institutional pattern \- it designates the frozen record of transductive operations, not their cause. Allagmatics is the operationalisation demand that the burn-rate concept enforces across all strata: every mediating structure must be cashable as an explicit transformation with a measurable throughput, or it is refused as a transcendent import. The CI Crosswalk template \- specifying variation, encounter, mediation, burn rate, and failure mode for each domain \- is the allagmatic demand systematised.
