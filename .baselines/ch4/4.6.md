## **4.6 The Plane of Immanence: Three Engines**

The Plane of Immanence is not a separate ontological stratum superimposed on the operators \- it *is* the operators read simultaneously, as a single field of constraints under which genesis, materiality, and normativity become tractable without recourse to miracle or tautology. No new beaker material is introduced here; no ninth operator is required. Three problems converge on the lattice \- how individuals precipitate, how their material regimes were constituted, how evaluative order emerges from a field's own expenditure-history \- and these three problems correspond to three engines whose resolution will occupy the remainder of the book under the same constraint that built the lattice: no miracles, no tautologies, no free lunches in ontology.

Each engine names a distinct mode of the triadic minimum's operation. Ontogenesis tracks the *forward* propagation of individuation through a metastable field \- the genesis of new structure. Hylogenesis tracks the *downward* archaeology of the constraint-floors that every individuation presupposes \- the constitution of the material regimes within which genesis occurs. Normogenesis tracks the *recursive* evaluation of regimes by their own topological features \- the emergence of evaluative order from expenditure-history rather than from transcendent legislation. The three are not separable processes occurring in sequence but distinguishable aspects of a single compositional dynamic, analytically isolable only because each foregrounds a different question the lattice must answer.

Before the engines are posed, the lattice must demonstrate its diagnostic apparatus at the site where every operator earned its keep. The CI Crosswalk \- the allagmatic demand systematised \- reads at the sodium acetate front as follows:

* Variation: the polymorphic underdetermination of the crystalline outcome \- multiple lattice geometries accessible from the same molecular species under variable boundary conditions.  
* Encounter: the seed crystal overpowering the dissolved state \- each molecular lock-in as a resolution of competing energetic demands whose differential determines which regime prevails.  
* Mediation: the lattice geometry channelling subsequent incorporations \- the propagating front's coherent advance, the sedimented regularity each resolved layer bequeaths to the next.  
* Burn rate: the enthalpy of crystallisation \- the rate of constitutive dissipation at which the system converts metastable potential into determinate structure, expressible as *dS*\_i/*dt* \> 0\.  
* Collapse: regime-destructive dissipation past the dissolution horizon \- the lattice degrading, the constraint-architecture failing the cessation-test (Ch. 3 §3.5): remove the condition and the stratum degrades.

Every subsequent domain the book enters \- biological morphogenesis, cognitive event-formation, institutional constitution \- will be tested through this same diagnostic: specify what counts as variation, encounter, and mediation in the domain; identify the local burn rate and what collapse looks like; name which engine is primary; produce evidence of Thirdness \- or the extension is refused.
