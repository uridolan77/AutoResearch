## **4.1.3 The Deficit**

The sealed room now has furniture \- operative rules, intrinsic differentiation, nested constraint-hierarchies \- but its burn rate is zero. Every load is pre-borne, every stress absorbed by the initial divine settlement, no historical transformation possible. What the architecture lacks is encounter and the mediation encounter deposits \- the passage from one regime of individuation to another through genuine collision, real expenditure, and immanent governance. Peirce begins the conversion.
