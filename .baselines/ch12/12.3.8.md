## §12.3.8 — The Flame and the Fuel

The three predictions are not news to anyone who has worked in a public defender's office, a regulatory enforcement division, or a corporate compliance department. They are news to a philosophy of mind that has treated the self as if its structure were determined by the phenomenology inside the skull rather than by the enforcement apparatus that the skull is held answerable to. The phenomenology is real. The PSM is real. The narrative self is real. They are all real in exactly the way a flame is real: as the visible face of a process whose fuel is elsewhere.

The fuel is the metabolic subsidy paid by the polity to keep the Canon readable, the Witness honest, the Replicator productive, and the Renormaliser solvent. When any of the four goes, the Symbolic Self does not disappear; it deforms in structurally specific ways. The next four sections audit each of the four deformations against a named case, with publicly available data, at real institutions, for recent and current periods.
